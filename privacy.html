Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

1. Data processed

The application may process account identifiers, authorization tokens, clip metadata, video files, titles, subtitles, publication status, and technical logs required to provide its features.

2. How data is used

Data is used only to retrieve authorized Twitch clips, generate vertical videos, display publication status, and publish selected content to accounts authorized by the user.

3. Local storage

The application is designed to run locally. Video files, configuration files, tokens, logs, and processing history may be stored on the user's own computer. Users are responsible for protecting access to their device and local files.

4. Third-party platforms

When a user connects Twitch, TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, or another supported service, data may be transmitted to that platform through its official API. Each platform processes data under its own privacy policy and terms.

5. Data sharing and sale

Personal data is not sold. Data is not shared with advertisers. Data is only sent to connected platforms when necessary to perform an action requested or enabled by the user.

6. Retention and deletion

Local data remains on the user's device until the user deletes the relevant files or uninstalls the application. Users may revoke platform access from the platform's account settings and delete locally stored tokens and processing history.

7. Security

Reasonable technical measures are used, but no system is completely secure. Users must not share client secrets, access tokens, refresh tokens, or configuration files containing credentials.

8. Your choices

You may stop using the application, disable publication features, revoke connected-account access, and delete locally stored data at any time.
